# StoryboardFlow Alpha — Tester Instructions

Welcome to the StoryboardFlow alpha program. Thank you for helping us build a better storyboard tool.

---

## What is StoryboardFlow?

StoryboardFlow is a web app for creating visual storyboards — organizing shots across pages, applying themes, and exporting to PDF or PNG. Your feedback directly shapes what we build next.

---

## What we need from you

1. **One focused session (45–60 min)** following the alpha test script we provide.
2. **Honest feedback** — confusion and criticism are valuable.
3. **A short Google Form** after your session (~5 min).
4. **Optional:** A 25–35 minute follow-up interview.

---

## Getting started

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Open the StoryboardFlow link from your invite email |
| 2 | Read the **Alpha Test Script** (`alpha-test-script.md` or PDF from coordinator) |
| 3 | Complete the tasks at your own pace; think aloud if a facilitator is present |
| 4 | Submit the **Google Form** linked in your invite |
| 5 | Optional: book an interview using the scheduler link |

---

## Technical requirements

- **Browser:** Latest Chrome, Firefox, or Edge (desktop)
- **Connection:** Stable internet (offline mode may be available; sync behavior is under test)
- **Account:** Use the sign-up method specified in your invite (email or Google)

---

## Privacy and data

- We collect **product usage analytics** via PostHog (e.g. when you create a project or export). Event names are documented in our repository; we do not sell your data.
- **Form and interview responses** are stored in our internal Product Discovery workspace (Notion) for triage.
- Do not include confidential client work in storyboards unless you have permission.
- You may use a pseudonym (e.g. "Tester A") in forms if you prefer.

---

## What to focus on

We're especially interested in:

- Can you create a project and add your **first shot** without help?
- Can you find **export** and get a usable PDF or PNG?
- Is it clear whether your work is **saved** (guest vs signed-in)?
- Anything that felt **slow**, **broken**, or **confusing**

---

## Reporting urgent issues

If you hit a **blocker** (data loss, cannot sign in, export completely fails):

1. Note the time and what you clicked.
2. Email or Slack the alpha coordinator (contact in your invite).
3. Still submit the form so we can correlate with analytics.

---

## What happens after your session

1. We review your feedback within one week.
2. Themes appear in our weekly product review.
3. Fixes and improvements are tracked in GitHub Issues.
4. We may invite you to a **second round** after changes ship.

---

## Thank you

Your time helps us build StoryboardFlow for real storyboard creators. Questions? Reply to your invite email or contact the alpha coordinator listed there.
